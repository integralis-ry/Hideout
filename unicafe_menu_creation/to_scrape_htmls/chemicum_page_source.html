Welcome to lunch!

UniCafe serves on all campuses. Eat in the restaurant or grab a takeaway lunch to go.

Takeaway sales of student meals to continue until further notice. In the future, the meal subsidy can only be used for one meal per day.

Where to have lunch in the summer?

UniCafe will be serving customers on all four campuses this summer. The UniCafes open all summer are Biokeskus, Chemicum, Kaivopiha and Meilahti. All UniCafe lunch menus the latest opening hours can be found on the mobile app menu.unicafe.fi and website.
